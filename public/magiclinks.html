Contrary to what the name implies, Magic Links are not, in fact, magical. They're just code. But they do make signing in fast, easy and secure (and maybe a bit delightful).

With a Magic Link workflow the site asks you only for your email address, not a password. Then the application prepares and sends you an email containing a link with an embedded token. The token tells us who you are and sends it directly to your inbox. Further, the link is valid only for a short time, and it cannot be used more than once. You open the email, click the link, and you're in!

If you're a returning registered user, we already know you and we load your settings. If we've never seen you before or if you signed out, we ask you to enter a token that we text to you. That token lets us link you with the organization you're meant to be part of, with your Role in that organization already set.

And note: The system "remembers" who last logged in on this computer. It will use that information next time to log you in automatically. If you don't want this to occur, be sure to Sign Out.
